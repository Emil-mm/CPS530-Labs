Chittagong, Bangladesh

Chittagong is the second largest city in Bangladesh and contains one of the world's oldest sea ports.

They have rich biodiversity with mountains, forests, waterfalls, haor, bird sanctuary, and safari parks.

Their cusine is known for there seafood dish with there abundant suply of fresh seafood

Facts

In Chittagong, the Cox’s Bazar is the longest natural beach on the planet spanning over 120 kilometers long

Chittagong has a vibrant and colorful street market attracting tourists and locals for a vibrant shopping experience.

Chittagong offers many adventurous and adrenaline pumping activities, including:

  • Hiking
  • Rock Climbing
  • River Rafting
  • And Much More!

Cuisine

Mutton Curry

Mutton curry is a traditional Chittagong dish made for special occasions

Fish curry

Also known as maacher jhol which is most frequently made with freshwater carp

Mishti Doi

A popular desert in bangladesh, a sweet fermented yogurt made with milk and sugar

Atikka pitha

Sweet or savory snack made of cooked rice, sugar, milk and spices wrapped in banana leaves
